Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Founded in the heart of Sana'a, Lidien Ventures was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make clean energy accessible to every Yemeni household and business. What started as a small LPG distribution service has evolved into Yemen's leading sustainable energy provider.

Our founders, recognizing the urgent need for reliable energy infrastructure in Yemen, combined their expertise in renewable energy with a deep understanding of local communities' needs. Today, we proudly serve over 3,400 households across multiple governorates.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

2018

Foundation & First Steps

Lidien Ventures established in Sana'a with initial focus on LPG distribution to 50 households in the capital.

2019

Solar Initiative Launch

Expanded services to include solar energy systems, completing our first major solar installation for a rural school in Ibb.

2020

Biogas Innovation

Introduced biogas solutions, helping farming communities convert waste to energy while reducing environmental impact.

2021

Water Access Program

Launched solar-powered borehole initiative, providing clean water access to over 10 rural communities.

2022

Regional Expansion

Extended operations to 15 governorates, establishing regional service centers and local partnerships.

2024

Milestone Achievement

Reached 3,400 satisfied customers and launched our community education program on sustainable energy practices.
